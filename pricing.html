Frequently Asked Questions

We know that getting started with a new tool can be intimidating, but we're here to help.

Get Started
How does the platform work?

Our email marketing platform is a user-friendly tool that allows you to create, send, and track professional email campaigns. You can use our templates and design tools to create emails that match your brand, or you can create your own from scratch. You can also segment your email list and set up automated emails based on specific actions or events.

Why should I choose MIW?

Our platform is designed to be easy to use, even if you don't have any design or coding experience. We offer a wide range of templates and design tools to help you create professional-quality emails that match your brand. Our advanced analytics and tracking tools allow you to see how your campaigns are performing and make data-driven decisions. We also offer excellent customer support to help you every step of the way.

How do I create and send an email campaign?

Creating and sending an email campaign with our platform is easy! Simply use our email designer or choose from a selection of customisable templates to create your email. You can then add your content, images, and any other elements you want to include. Once you're happy with your email, you can use our campaign creator to set up your campaign, specify your target audience, and
schedule your send.

How do I import and manage my email list?

You can import your email list into our platform by uploading a CSV file or by connecting to your CRM system or other tools. You can then use our list management tools to segment your list, add or
remove subscribers, and more.

Can I personalise my emails with custom fields?

Yes, you can use merge tags to insert personalised content, such as the subscriber's name or location, into your emails. This is a great way to make your emails more relevant and engaging for
your audience.

How do I track the performance of my email campaigns?

You can track the performance of your email campaigns using our advanced analytics tools. These tools provide detailed tracking and reporting, including information on open rates, click-through rates, bounces, and more.

Can I segment my email list for targeted messaging?

Yes, you can use our segmentation tools to divide your email list into specific groups based on criteria such as location, interests, or behaviour. This allows you to send targeted emails with customised
content that is relevant to each group.

Do you offer any templates or design assistance?

Yes, we offer a variety of customisable templates to choose from, as well as a user-friendly email designer that makes it easy to create professional-quality emails. If you need additional design assistance, our support team is available to help.

What is your pricing model?

Our pricing is based on the number of emails you send and the features you need. We offer a range of plans to suit the needs of businesses of all sizes and budgets.
